About Crown Fountain

The Jay Pritzker Pavilion is a bandshell located in Millennium Park, Chicago, designed by the arhitect Frank Gehry and inaugurated in 2004. Pritzker Pavilion serves as the centerpiece for Millennium Park and is the home of the Grant Park Symphony Orchestra and many other festivals.

What makes it special:Jay Pritzker Pavilion is special because it combines spectacular modern architecture with state-of-the-art acoustic technology.

Fun Fact:Jay Pritzker Pavilion legally, it was not classified as a building, but as a "work of art".
