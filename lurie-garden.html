About Lurie Garden

Lurie Garden is a 2.5-acre garden located at the southern end of Millennium Park in the Loop area of Chicago in Cook County, Illinois. Designed by Gustafson Guthrie Nichol, Piet Oudolf, and Robert Israel, it opened on July 16, 2004.

What makes it special:The garden symbolizes the past, present, and future of Chicago through its two contrasting areas, Dark Plate and Light Plate, which represent the city's history and rebirth.

Fun Fact:Lurie Garden is built on the roof of an underground parking garage in Millennium Park.
